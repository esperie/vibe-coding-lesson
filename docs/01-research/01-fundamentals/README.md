# Cybersecurity Fundamentals Research

> Background research documentation for government cybersecurity unit preparation
>
> Last Updated: January 2026

## Overview

This documentation provides comprehensive foundational knowledge for professionals joining a government cybersecurity unit. The materials cover core security concepts, the current threat landscape, major defense frameworks, and career essentials specific to government cybersecurity roles.

## Quick Statistics (2025-2026)

### Threat Landscape
- **Global cybercrime cost**: $10.5 trillion projected for 2025
- **Average data breach cost**: $4.44 million (2025 IBM report)
- **Ransomware in breaches**: 44% of all breaches (37% increase YoY)
- **Phishing attacks**: 1.13 million in Q2 2025 alone
- **AI-powered phishing**: 82.6% of phishing emails now use AI-generated content

### Workforce
- **Unfilled cybersecurity jobs globally**: 3.5 million
- **Projected job growth (2024-2034)**: 29% for Information Security Analysts
- **Top Secret clearance salary premium**: ~$20,000-$25,000

### Key Developments
- NIST CSF 2.0 released with new "Govern" function
- NIST SP 1800-35 Zero Trust Architecture guidance finalized (June 2025)
- NIST SP 800-61r3 Incident Response guide updated (April 2025)
- MITRE ATT&CK v18 released (October 2025) with Detection Strategies
- OWASP Top 10:2025 released with new supply chain and exception handling categories

## Learning Path Recommendations

### Week 1-2: Foundation
1. Start with [01-core-concepts.md](./01-core-concepts.md) to understand fundamental principles
2. Focus on CIA Triad, Defense in Depth, and Zero Trust concepts

### Week 3-4: Threat Understanding
1. Study [02-threat-landscape.md](./02-threat-landscape.md) thoroughly
2. Pay special attention to nation-state actors and APT groups
3. Understand current attack vectors and trends

### Week 5-6: Frameworks and Standards
1. Deep dive into [03-defense-frameworks.md](./03-defense-frameworks.md)
2. Focus on NIST CSF 2.0 and MITRE ATT&CK as primary frameworks
3. Understand how frameworks complement each other

### Week 7-8: Career Preparation
1. Review [04-career-essentials.md](./04-career-essentials.md)
2. Understand security clearance processes and timeline
3. Study incident response procedures and legal requirements
