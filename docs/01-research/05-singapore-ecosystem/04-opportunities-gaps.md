## Key Gaps and Recommendations

### Identified Gaps

| Gap | Impact | Priority |
|-----|--------|----------|
| **Cloud Security Expertise** | Organizations struggle with cloud migration security | High |
| **AI/ML Security Skills** | Cannot secure or leverage AI properly | High |
| **Zero Trust Implementation** | Slow adoption of modern architectures | High |
| **OT Security Specialists** | Critical infrastructure at risk | Medium-High |
| **Threat Intelligence** | Limited attribution capability | Medium |
| **Executive Cyber Leadership** | Boards lack cyber expertise | Medium |

### Recommendations for Individuals

1. **Pursue In-Demand Certifications**: Focus on cloud and AI security
2. **Build Practical Experience**: Labs, CTFs, bug bounties
3. **Specialize**: OT, cloud, or AI security niches
4. **Network**: Join AiSP, attend SICW/GovWare
5. **Leverage Government Programs**: SkillsFuture, SG Cyber Talent

### Recommendations for Organizations

1. **Invest in Training**: Despite budget pressures, training ROI is high
2. **Partner with Academia**: NUS, NTU research collaborations
3. **Engage Government Programs**: CyberBoost, CyberCall for innovation
4. **Build Apprenticeship Pipelines**: Address entry-level shortage
5. **Focus on Retention**: Address burnout and attrition
